Scripted in Al Qaeda Ink

Political Thriller • Debut Novel

When a New York Times reporter receives an unmarked envelope promising to expose a suppressed international crisis involving Al Qaeda, a U.S. president, and Hollywood's biggest reality show, he's skeptical—until the source logs into a secure channel and begins to speak.

At the height of its fame, the hit series Marooned is targeted by a terrorist cell led by Ja'far Bahili al-Hasan—a former child witness to unspeakable brutality, now a hardened operative carrying out the final directive of a slain jihadist leader. His target: the cast and crew of the show, and the millions watching from home.

As filming wraps on the remote island set, the remaining contestants are ambushed under cover of night and taken to an abandoned military bunker deep in the Colombian jungle. There, the rules of the game are rewritten. Cameras still roll—but this time, the stakes are survival, and the audience is no longer in control.

Gripping, cinematic, and disturbingly plausible, Scripted in Al Qaeda Ink pulls back the curtain on a world where entertainment, extremism, and politics collide—and the most dangerous weapon is the illusion of control.

Albatross: A Paul Banter Story

Thriller • Follow-up Novel

Some ghosts don't haunt—they hunt.

Detroit's a hard city. Paul Banter's harder.

When a sultry voice offers him cash for a late-night meet, Banter expects trouble. He gets a fresh corpse, a brutal setup, and a beating that leaves him bleeding in an alley. Now he's the prime suspect in a murder tied to the case that ruined his career—and nearly killed him.

With the cops on one side, the mob on the other, and a dead partner's shadow chasing every step, Banter's got one shot to blow the case open before it buries him for good.

Everyone's got secrets. Someone's killing to keep them.

For Banter, the truth isn't just dangerous—it's personal.

About the Author

G.T. Reoch is a distinguished author whose compelling narratives have captivated readers across multiple genres. With a keen eye for character development and intricate storytelling, Reoch creates immersive worlds that resonate with readers long after the final page.

Drawing inspiration from diverse experiences and extensive research, G.T. Reoch's works span historical fiction, contemporary drama, and speculative fiction. Each novel is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, creating authentic characters and believable worlds that transport readers into extraordinary journeys.

When not writing, G.T. Reoch enjoys exploring historical sites, reading works by fellow authors, and connecting with readers who share a passion for well-crafted stories. The author believes in the power of literature to bridge cultures, spark imagination, and illuminate the human experience.
